Allison Morgan

I’m a UX & Human Factors researcher who plans and runs end-to-end human-subjects studies across lab, field, and remote settings. I combine qualitative methods with multimodal data (eye-tracking, wearables/physiology, XR) to turn complex signals into clear, decision-ready insights for interfaces and products.

I partner closely with design, product, and engineering to scope research questions, build reliable ops and instrumentation pipelines (recruiting, IRB, QA, data governance), and deliver findings that improve usability, safety, and overall experience.

I earned my B.S. in Cognitive & Behavioral Neuroscience from UC San Diego. I’m focused on inclusive design, hardware evaluation, and responsible AI with demonstrated impact in real settings.
